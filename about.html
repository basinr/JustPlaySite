We connect you to a game, anytime, anywhere

JustPlay is the premier app for finding, creating, and joining pick-up games, tailored to your interests and location. JustPlay empowers you to browse and participate in games happening near you, no matter where you are. Make new connections and find a community of individuals with the same goal as you - get out there and play.

  • Filter the games you see based on the sport and proximity to your current location
  • Create your own games - both private and public - and invite your friends to join
  • Keep track of time, number of players attending, max players, and location all on one screen
  • Enter locations with manual input and Google location search
  • Connect with friends on Facebook and bring them into your games
  • Plan your games ahead of time or find a game and just play
